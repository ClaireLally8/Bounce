My Story

About Me

Travel has always been a passion of mine. In 2016, I decided to pack my bags, fill my van and travel the length and breadth of Ireland. Documenting some of my favourite spots along the way, and ensuring I spread these tips with everyone. My passion for travel has taken me around the world, but my heart always comes back to Ireland.

How it happened

In 2016, I risked everything. I left my job, but started a new one, working remotely. Allowing me to travel the country. Hiiking and sight-seening has been a passion since I was a child. Being outdoors, the peace, the escape from reality. Now, with everything, I get to do this everyday, for the foreseeable furture. As long as I want.

What you'll find

Travel Ireland is just that. It's my travel diary. There's nothing more I want to do than share my travel experieneces with others & what better place to start, but with my own home. Everything you need to have the best adventure, is in your backgarden. From, the best hiking spots, to sightseeing points, to places to camp. You'll find it all here.
